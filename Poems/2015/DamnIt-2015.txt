‘Damn it’ he thought.

‘And damn it again.’

His tea cup flew through the air and clattered against a wall.

‘Impulsive action’, he thought.
‘Look at the mess that’s been made.’

He sat up, pushed himself from his seat, walked over to the wall,
bent over and picked up his cup. Upon returning to his seat he
poured himself a cup of tea.

‘Damn it.’
